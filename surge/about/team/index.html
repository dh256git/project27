Who we are?

We are a small but committed family business with great friends and advisors. View who is in our core team, who gives us advice, and who volunteered to give their time.

The core team

Project27 Skills is managed by this awesome core team.

Daniel Hajas - Project27 Skills Director of Research and Innovation

As a co-founder of PCG, Daniel's main contribution is setting the vision, the roadmap, and the community culture. He leads on the activities of Project27 Solutions; however, he also loves getting hands-on in any role within Project27 SKills.
View Daniel's profile

Danielle Garratt - Project27 Skills Director of Community Engagement

Danielle is taking a lead on our work with learning disability. She is the head of Project27 Skills; however, she also contributes to research and innovation projects.

The advisory team

We wouldn't be where we are today, without the invaluable advice and guidance of our advisors.

Robert Cobden - Systems Advisor

Rob has been a Research Engineer since 2016, at the Sussex Computer-Human Interaction Lab, where Daniel met him. Gradually, Rob became one of Daniel's most trusted friends. Rob is a skilful coder, and maker of things. His role is to review our systems and highlight any inconsistencies or weaknesses.
