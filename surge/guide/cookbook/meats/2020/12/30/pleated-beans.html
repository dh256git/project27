Pleated green bean casserole

Layer after layer of goodness. This is one beany recipe.

Not keen on reading?

We thought this video by Hungarian Home Cooking is a good alternative of this post.

Ingredients and kitchenware

The following recommendation usually serves 5 of us. Make sure you have enough to share with your pals.

Before you start, check that you have the following ingredients.

Ingredient Quantity Unit
pork (minced) 500 g
cooking onions 1 piece
rice 125 g
cooked green beans 2 jars
sour cream 300 ml
bread crumbs 100 g
lard 1 tbsp
seasoning: -salt -pepper -paprika -Vegeta to your taste -

You will also have things to wash up afterwards. The dishes and appliances to have at hand are:

  1. two saucepan, and a wooden spoon;
  2. a baking tray;
  3. a chopping board;

Preparation and cooking time

It usually takes us 50 minutes to prepare this meal. No rush though, takes as long as it takes.

Every Project27 Skills recipe takes only 3 key steps to prepare. The rest is a matter of creativity..

Step 1: Play music in the kitchen. I recommend this playlist.

Step 2: Pour a glass of drink for the chef.

Step 3: Get cooking, get creative! Here is how we do it:

Steps

  1. Start boiling the rice.
  2. While the rice cooks, start preparing the minced stew. For this, chop the onions finely, and fry them on lard. When the onions soften, add paprika, according to your taste.
  3. Now add the minced meat to the stew base and stir it with the paprika covered onions. Add the vegetable stock, preferably in powder form. But you can use a stock cube dissolved in ~20 ml water.
  4. When the stew seems to be ready, add the boiled rice and stir it in.
  5. Now grease the baking tray with a little oil. Spread the green beans at the bottom. It should cover the bottom in a coherent layer.
  6. Load the stew onto the beans, and form a nice layer with it too.
  7. On top of the stew layer, spread the sour cream, such that it covers all the meat. This is when you can add extra seasoning, such as more salt, pepper, or as I did, a bit of garlic.
  8. Finally, top it up with another layer of green beans.
  9. The finishing touch is to add the bread crumbs on top. Potentially, you can make a mix of bread crumbs, cheese and seasoning to sprinkle on top.
