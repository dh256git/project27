Welcome to Project27 Skills :
The skill studio for disabled talents

We create personalised pathways of experiential training and professional development opportunities for blind and learning-disabled individuals. Through our community-driven learning resources and co-working platform, we provide a safe-space and tailored support to help members develop valuable skills, find their passions, and succeed in the job market, such that they can grow personally and professionally at the pace that suits them.

What we do

Our community services and fields of interest are complementary to the value created by mentorship organisations, parent support groups, and training or employment support services.

Our fields of interest

We focus on developing analytical and digital skills that matter to blind people interested in science & technology, for example studying mathematics, practicing web design, or working in accounting.

In addition, we also develop social and media skills that matter to neurodiverse people interested in a joyful lifestyle & media.

Our community services

We offer varying levels of engagement and support for different stages of self-development. Our community services are tailoured to the interaction needs of individuals, and their level of disability awareness or learning style.

Certifying work experience - Join our community!

We support blind, learning disabled, and any non-disabled industry professional member of our community with identifying their passions, strengths, and areas of development in small teams; certifying every step of their personal and professional growth during active participation in building Project27.

Providing expert mentorship - Book ad-hoc Support!

Book a one-time consultation or a series of mentorship sessions with our experts to address your questions about blindness or learning disability. We offer tailored advice for disabled individuals, parents and teachers, as well as employers of disabled staff. No membership required.

Publishing learning resources - Study our Guide!

We enable blind or learning disabled people to learn and develop new skills in science, technology, lifestyle, and media on their own, through a collection of accessible learning resources.

Sharing lived experience - Read our Stories!

We share our lived experience of social participation with anyone in doubt of the incredible achievements disabled people are capable of bringing about at work, at home, and anywhere in-between.
