Research and innovation portfolio

All of the solutions in our portfolio have been co-designed with researchers, and the projects are grounded in peer reviewed science. To maximise impact, we are working with businesses on diffusing innovation that works.

Project Olli

Olli is an open-source library for converting data visualizations into accessible text structures for screen reader users. Starting with an existing visualization specification created with a supported toolkit, Olli produces a keyboard-navigable tree view with descriptions at varying levels of detail. Users can explore these structures both to get an initial overview, and to dive into the data in more detail.

Learn more about Olli

Project Upset

This project aims to enhance the accessibility and usability of the UpSet visualisation technique, widely used in biomedical research. It will address challenges faced by low-vision and blind users in reading and creating UpSet plots. Key objectives include developing accessible workflows and alt-text templates, and improving the interactive, web-based version for non-coders.

Learn more about Upset

Project Groundwork

Groundwork, led by This New Ground, aims to preserve and archive the heritage of people with learning disabilities in Portsmouth. By filling gaps in historical records, this co-designed project amplifies their voices, challenges historical views, and ensures their stories are accessible through permanent archives and exhibitions.
